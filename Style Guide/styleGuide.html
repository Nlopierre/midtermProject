Debt Bidder Style guide

This style guide is to be used as a reference to maintain visual consistency when creating new, different webpages for this website.

Strategy

Debt Bidder's purpose is to encourage good financial practices by facilitating tasks such as renegotiating a debt. Since we like to watch our customers grow, it is important for us to provide advices and tools that can help them plan and build a better tomorrow. To do that, Debt Bidder simplifies and streamlines processes that normally take days to accomplish, and creates a direct bridge between lender and borrower. As an organization focusing on taking care of financial responsibilities, we provide our customers with a safe and easy platform to manage their debts.

Code Conventions

Naming Conventions

When it comes to coding, we prefer to use words-with-dashes. DO NOT write them with capital cases. For example, when inserting an attribute to a link, do it this way:

!--Avoid camelCase!--

href="this-is-the-correct-way.html"

Attributes

Include the alt attribute for all images. Since we are targeting a very diverse group of people, we need to make sure that the Screen Readers can work with our websites nicely.
